About

A digital consultancy built for the enterprise, run like a product studio.

Based in Johannesburg, we work with organisations who need agency-grade design and engineering-grade rigour in the same team.

Our story

Plutus Andromeda Group started as a small team of designers and engineers frustrated by how often digital projects in South Africa were treated as a cost centre rather than a growth lever. We set out to build a consultancy that could operate at the same calibre as the design studios our clients admired internationally, while understanding the regulatory and operational reality of doing business here.

We work across mining, and are building relationships across banking, healthcare, telecommunications and government — sectors where the cost of getting digital experience wrong is measured in more than lost conversions. That context shapes everything we design.

We remain intentionally small: focused enough that every project has senior attention from strategy through to launch, rather than being handed off to whoever's available.

What we hold ourselves to

Clarity over cleverness

We choose the solution that's easiest to understand and maintain, not the one that's most impressive to demo.

Evidence over opinion

Design decisions are grounded in research and data. Taste informs execution — it doesn't replace evidence.

Ownership, not handoff

We stay accountable to outcomes past delivery, not just the artefacts we hand over on the way out.

Rigour at every scale

A landing page and a mining platform get the same discipline in process, even if the stakes look different.

Why Plutus

Craft and rigour, in equal measure.

We combine agency-grade design with the engineering discipline enterprise platforms demand.

Strategy Before Pixels

We don't open a design file until we understand the business problem. Every engagement starts with discovery, not deliverables.

Built to Scale

Architecture and design systems that hold up as your organisation grows — not fragile builds that need rebuilding in eighteen months.

Enterprise Ready

Security, accessibility and governance built in from day one.

Human-Centred Design

Every decision is tested against real users, not internal assumptions. We design for the people who'll actually use it.
